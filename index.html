Reaching The Unreached India

  • About
  • Mission
  • Vision
India is a country with more than 60% rural population and most of the students are unable to get quality education. We are working with private schools in rural India to build Model Learning Centres for school education. Through this platform we are helping the unreached population of our country to get good education and make India a better nation.
Helping 10000+ students to get a good education by getting them sponsors and mentors.
To empower the future generation of rural India.

What's here for you!

Sponsor Education for Needy Students

Regular growth report

We will provide you with a quarterly progress report of your sponsored child as well as an annual progress report at the end of the session. We will also release an overall progess report of the program yearly comprising all the activities and finances.

Direct connectivity to Student

When you sponsor a child, you'll receive your sponsored child's photo, his/her personal story and other contact details by mail in approximately 10 days. We will also connect the child with you on a video conference monthly after sharing his/her growth report.

Become a Mentor

As you are sponsoring one child for his/her education. You can extend your support to him/her by mentoring or counseling them regularly. You can further extend you mentoring support to other students as well.

One Day School Visit

We will love to offer you the opportunity to visit the schools for one day and interact with your sponsored child and other students.
